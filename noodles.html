Garlic Noodle Recipe

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5 - 10 min 10 - 15 min 15 - 25 min
Jump To Nutrition Facts

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of spaghetti or egg noodles
  • 3 table spoons of unsalted butter
  • 6 to 8 finely minced cloves of garlic
  • 2 table spoons of oyster sauce (or hoison sauce for vegetarian option)
  • 1 table spoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tea spoon of fish sauce (Optional)
  • 1 tea spoon of sugar
  • 1/4 tea spoon of ground black pepper
  • 2 table spoons of grated Parmesan cheese (Optional)
  • Chopped scallions or parsley


    • Instructions

    1. Cook The Noodles:

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

    • 2. Prepare The Sauce:

  • In a small bowl, mix oyster sauce (or hoison sauce for vegetarian option), soy sauce, fish sauce (if using), sugar, and black pepper. Set aside.

    • 3. Sauté garlic:

  • In a large skillet or wok, melt the butter over meduim heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until frgrant and golden, about 1-2 minutes. Be carful not to burn the garlic.

    • 4. Combine Noodles and Sauce:

  • Add the cooked noodles to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture. Toss well to coat the noodles evenly.

    • 5. Finish and Serve

  • Remove from heat, garnish with Parmesan cheese and scallions or parsley, and serve immediately.
